Research and Teaching

You can find my CV here.

Understanding Crowdlearning
With Isabel Valera and Manuel Gomez Rodriguez, I am developing models to understand how learning happens on Crowdlearning sites, such as Stack Overflow and Wikipedia.
  • "On Crowdlearning: How do People Learn in the Wild?", oral presentation at Workshop on Machine Learning for Education at NIPS (2016). Link to talk.
  • "Uncovering the dynamics of Crowdlearning and the Value of Knowledge", oral presentation at WSDM (2017).
Recurrent Marked Temporal Point Processes
With Nan Du, Hanjun Dai, Rakshit Trivedis, Manuel Gomez-Rodriguez, and Le Song, I developed am model which uses recurrent neural networks to model point processes, yielding impressive predictive results.
  • "Recurrent marked temporal point processes: Embedding event history to vector", Poster presetned at KDD (2016). Link to paper.
RedQueen: An Online Algorithm for Smart Broadcasting
With Ali Zarezade, and Manuel Gomez Rodriquez, I discover a simple algorithm for keeping your posts visible on your follower's feeds.
  • "RedQueen: An Online Algorithm for Smart Broadcasting in Social Networks" (to appear) Oral presentation at WSDM (2017). Link to paper.
